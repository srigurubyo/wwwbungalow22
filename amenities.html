Policies and Guidelines

Check-In & Check-Out

Check-In: 1:00 p.m.

Check-Out: 11:00 a.m.

Guest Requirements

Valid government ID is required (Aadhar, Passport, DL).

Foreigners must provide passport & valid visa.

Occupancy

Each room accommodates 2 adults & 2 kids below 10 years.

Kitchen Timings

Breakfast: 8:00 – 10:00 a.m.

Lunch: 1:00 – 3:00 p.m.

Dinner: 7:30 – 9:30 p.m.
