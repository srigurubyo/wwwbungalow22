Welcome to BUNGALOW 22 - The Estate Villa!

A serene villa nestled amidst the lush forests and tea plantations of Valparai. Offering a perfect blend of comfort and nature, we provide well-furnished rooms with modern amenities, delicious home-cooked meals, and a tranquil retreat for families, couples, and nature explorers. With guided walks and breathtaking views, Bungalow 22 is your ideal getaway for relaxation and exploration. Escape the city’s hustle and immerse yourself in the beauty of Valparai with us!
