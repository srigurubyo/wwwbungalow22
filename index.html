Welcome to BUNGALOW 22 - The Estate Villa!

A serene villa nestled amidst the lush forests and tea plantations of Valparai. Offering a perfect blend of comfort and nature, we provide well-furnished rooms with modern amenities, delicious home-cooked meals, and a tranquil retreat for families, couples, and nature explorers.
